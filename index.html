Tips from Hooter!

Addition is a basic operation in mathematics that allows you to combine two or more numbers into one larger number.

You are able to use this everyday! From adding costs at the grocery store, to the time it takes you to get places.

To do this, you simply bring together the two numbers and find how much they make together.

Example: If you have 3 computers and you get 2 more, you would have 5 computers total!

Addition is a quick and easy way to figure out the totals to anything in your daily life, so practice is important!
